# Cybersecurity Awareness Study

## Overview
This project presents an academic research study on cybersecurity risks in business applications. It focuses on identifying common vulnerabilities and proposing strategies to improve data protection and reduce security threats.

## Objectives
- Identify common cybersecurity threats in business systems
- Analyze vulnerabilities in digital business applications
- Raise cybersecurity awareness
- Recommend strategies for improving security and protecting sensitive data

## Key Areas of Research
- Cybersecurity risks in business applications
- Data protection methods
- Risk mitigation strategies
- Security awareness for organizations

## Solutions Proposed
- Implement strong authentication mechanisms
- Regular security audits and monitoring
- Employee cybersecurity training
- Data encryption and secure communication protocols

## Importance of the Study
Cybersecurity is critical for modern businesses that rely on digital systems. This research highlights major risks and offers practical solutions to help organizations strengthen their security posture.
